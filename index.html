Does Your Dog Think the Litter Box is a Snack Shack?

There are many reasons why dogs may eat cat stool: boredom, instinct, or most often nutritional deficiency. Typically, cat feces contain a high amount of protein and fat combined with an odor that is very appealing to dogs. Snacking on cat feces is a nasty habit that could lead to health issues if not corrected. Outta My Box™ is a natural solution, veterinarian formulated to help deter dogs from consuming cat stools.

If you have a dog that invades the litter box to snack on cat stools, then Outta My Box™ is the product for you!

No more worry and hassle of monitoring your dog and guarding your cat's little box. Plus, you'll welcome those doggie kisses again.
